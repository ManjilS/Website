Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate?

Students and enthusiasts passionate about building great products.

Team size?

2–5 members per team. Solo registration isn't allowed; if you sign up solo, we'll match you with a team.

Registration fee?

NRS 2000 per team, payable during onboarding.

When is the schedule shared?

Final schedule will be emailed to selected teams.

Meals and lunches?

Yes, all meals and lunches will be provided throughout the hackathon.

Entertainment?

Expect music, mini‑games, late‑night chill zones, and surprise activities to keep the vibes high.

Participant Essentials

Bring

Laptop, chargers, required accessories, and valid ID card.

Prepare

GitHub account, project idea, and team setup (2–5 members).

Onboarding

Pay the registration fee (NRS 2000) and follow emailed schedule.
