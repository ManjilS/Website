Frequently Asked Questions

What is the team size limit?

Teams can have 2-5 members. Individual participation is also allowed.

What should I bring?

Bring your laptop, chargers, and any hardware you might need. We'll provide food and drinks.

Is there a registration fee?

No, NECSprint is completely free to participate.

What are the judging criteria?

Projects will be judged on innovation, technical implementation, presentation, and potential impact.
